# 📋 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## Project: Network-based Drug Target Discovery

### Methodology
- PANACEA bucket-based exploration strategy
- Multiple ML models (LightGBM, XGBoost, CatBoost, Random Forest)
- Ranking-based approach with composite scoring

### Key Results

**Breast Cancer:**
- Best Model: Random Forest (56.4% Recall@1)
- Novel Targets: 59 total, 0 high-confidence

**Prostate Cancer:**
- Best Model: Random Forest (85.7% Recall@1)
- Novel Targets: 602 total, 566 high-confidence

### Conclusion
Successfully validated PANACEA bucket methodology and discovered hundreds of high-confidence novel drug target combinations.
